FCA expectations on operational resilience and AI governance mean firms need to know where their AI runs, who controls it, and what happens to the data that passes through it. Marigold gives you a clear, documentable answer to all three.

Compliance-relevant properties

01

UK data residency

Inference runs on private AWS infrastructure in London. Data does not leave the UK as part of inference, storage, or queuing. No cross-border transfer occurs.

02

No US sub-processors

No inference request reaches OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, or any other external provider. The model runs locally from weights held on UK infrastructure. Your AI supply chain has one link.

03

No training on inputs

Customer data passed to the inference API is not retained for training, fine-tuning, or any secondary purpose. Outputs are stored briefly for retrieval and then deleted.

04

Audit trail

Every inference job records status, timing, and identity within UK infrastructure. Access logs are retained in CloudWatch within the UK region. Queryable by job and by time window.

05

Inspectable models

All models are open-weight with publicly documented architectures and training data. No proprietary black-box model receives your data. Model provenance is fully traceable.

06

UK-registered company

Bay Information Systems is a UK-registered company. Contractual relationships are with a UK entity subject to UK law and UK regulatory jurisdiction.

FCA and operational resilience

Know your AI supply chain

PS22/3 and the FCA's evolving expectations on AI governance require firms to understand and document the third parties in their operational infrastructure. An AI inference provider is a material third party if it processes customer data or supports a business service.

Marigold's architecture is designed to be simple to document: one provider, one region, no sub-processors for inference, full audit logs. We cannot assess whether our service meets your specific FCA obligations -- that depends on your use case, your important business services mapping, and your compliance team's judgement. What we can provide is the technical detail to support that assessment.

Frequently asked

Does Marigold process data outside the UK?

No. Inference runs on private AWS infrastructure in London (UK). Data does not leave that region. No US-based model provider receives your data.

Does Marigold train on customer data?

No. Inference inputs are not retained for training, fine-tuning, or any other purpose. Outputs are stored briefly for retrieval and then deleted.

Can Marigold support our FCA operational resilience obligations?

Marigold provides audit logs and UK-resident infrastructure with no US sub-processors. Whether this satisfies your firm's specific obligations under PS22/3 or related rules depends on your use case and your compliance team's assessment. We are happy to discuss the architecture in detail to support that process.
