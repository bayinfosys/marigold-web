Get in touch Architecture detail

NHS trusts, CCGs, and public sector health organisations are adopting AI tooling and hitting the same wall: US-based providers cannot satisfy UK data residency requirements. Marigold is a UK company running UK infrastructure with no US model provider in the request path.

Data handling properties

01

UK data residency

Inference runs on private AWS infrastructure in London (UK). Data does not leave that region as part of inference, storage, or queuing. No cross-border transfer occurs.

02

No US sub-processors

No inference request reaches OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, or any other US-based model provider. The model runs locally from weights held on UK infrastructure.

03

No training on inputs

Inference inputs are not retained for training, fine-tuning, or any other secondary purpose. Outputs are stored briefly for retrieval and then deleted.

04

Open-weight models

Models run from publicly documented open-weight checkpoints. The weights, architecture, and behaviour of each model are inspectable. No proprietary black-box model receives your data.

05

Audit trail

Every inference job records status and timing within your region. Execution history is queryable. Access logs are retained in CloudWatch within the UK region.

06

UK-registered company

Bay Information Systems is a UK-registered company. Any contractual relationship is with a UK entity subject to UK law.

DSPT considerations

Designed with UK data residency requirements in mind

The NHS Data Security and Protection Toolkit requires that personal data is processed with documented controls and within appropriate jurisdictional boundaries. Marigold's architecture addresses the aspects of that requirement that relate to AI inference: where the data goes, who can access it, and whether it is retained.

We are not able to make a blanket claim that Marigold satisfies your organisation's DSPT obligations -- that assessment depends on your specific data types, use case, and information governance framework. What we can do is provide the technical detail your IG team needs to make that assessment, and discuss your requirements before any commitment is made.

Frequently asked

Does Marigold process data outside the UK?

No. Inference runs on private AWS infrastructure in London (UK) by default. Data does not leave that region as part of inference. No US-based model provider receives your data.

Does Marigold train on patient data or inference inputs?

No. Inference inputs are not retained for training, fine-tuning, or any other purpose. Outputs are stored briefly for retrieval and then deleted.

Is Marigold suitable for NHS DSPT requirements?

Marigold's architecture is designed to support UK data residency requirements. Whether it satisfies your organisation's specific DSPT obligations depends on your data types, use case, and your IG team's assessment. We are happy to discuss the technical architecture in detail to support that assessment before any commitment is made.

Is a data processing agreement available?

We are able to discuss data processing terms with organisations that have specific requirements. We do not currently have a standard DPA document ready to issue -- if you have requirements in this area, contact us and we will work through them with you.

Is Marigold on G-Cloud?

Not currently. If G-Cloud listing is a requirement for your procurement process, let us know -- understanding the demand will inform when we prioritise that registration.

What models are available?
